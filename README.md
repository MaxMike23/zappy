# Zappy
**Configurable Workflow OS for AV, Security, and Low Voltage Companies**

## What is Zappy?

Zappy is a SaaS CRM/ERP platform built specifically for AV, security, and low-voltage integration companies.

Unlike traditional field service software, Zappy focuses on standardizing and enforcing internal job execution workflows — not just managing customers and invoices.

Whether your business operates in residential, commercial, or live events environments, Zappy adapts to your company’s unique processes instead of forcing you into rigid software structures.

## What Does It Do? 

Zappy helps AV companies:

- Standardize internal job workflows
- Enforce structured job documentation
- Improve communication between office staff and field technicians
- Track projects, work orders, and technician time
- Configure workflow stages per division or service type
- Design custom AV systems to fit client needs
- Define required job note fields and completion requirements
- Track job note history to figure out what tasks are left 
- Maintain consistent operational standards across teams

The goal is to eliminate confusion in workflow, job site procedures, documentation, and troubleshooting — while increasing operational visibility for management. 

## What sets it apart?

Most CRM and ERP systems are built for general field services like for plumbing, HVAC, and electrical companies. Platforms like ServiceTitan, Jobber, and Salesforce excel at managing customers and billing — but they are not designed around the technical execution complexity of AV and low-voltage installations, programming, and commissioning.

Most AV companies often require:
- Structured rack build documentation and outlet considerations
- Cable labeling standards for room cable drops
- Network documentation and configuration tracking
- Device install locations and room labeing schema
- Programming and system testing checklists
- Device firmware update timelines to schedule on-site or remote service
- Commissioning verification steps and on-site testing punch lists
- AV system manuals and quick start guides
- Technician job notes to show work done each day
- Close out documentation towards the end of every project
- Client archive for any future recommendations or project updates

Zappy is designed to support and enforce these internal execution standards and others based on your companies standards aand procedures

Instead of forcing companies to change their workflow to match the software, Zappy allows organizations to design, enforce, and evolve their internal field workflows — without requiring consultants, developers, or expensive customization contracts.
